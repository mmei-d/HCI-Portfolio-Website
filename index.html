Who I Am

As a junior at Emory University studying Computer Science, I'm passionate about programming and aspire to become a software engineer. I've always been fascinated by the potential of technology to improve people's lives and my interest in coding has only grown stronger as I've taken on new challenges. Through my studies and personal projects, I've gained experience in various programming languages and technologies. I'm eager to continue learning and perfecting my skills as I work towards building innovative and impactful software solutions. Overall, I'm driven by my passion for technology and my desire to create solutions that make a real difference in the world.

Past Work Experience

While I was a freshman at Emory, I has the opportunity to work for a startup called "Kino," where I gained my first product design experiences. Throughout the journey, I learned how to build wireframes and prototypes with Figma. This past year, I was an intern for Emory's Computer Science Department, where I worked on product design and development for Emora Chat, an AI chatbot for Emory students. This upcoming summer, I'll be an software engineer intern at Goldman Sachs in New York City.

HCI Experience

Through HCI, I gained valuable insights into the relationship between technology and the human experience. I learned how to design digital interfaces that are intuitive, user-friendly, and accessible to a diverse range of users. I've delved into topics such as user research, usability testing, and interface design patterns. I've worked on design projects ranging from website design to data visualization and even physical prototyping.
